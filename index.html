Hi, I'm Aarush.

Computing Science Student & Accessibility Engineer

I am a student of the subtle and beautiful arts of computing science, exploring software engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and systems architecture at the University of Alberta.

Technology intersects with almost every aspect of modern life. For me, it is the primary mechanism I use to navigate and reshape the world. Driven by a lifelong journey of breaking systemic barriers, I believe that technology, if utilized with thoughtfulness, has the potential to dismantle exclusion. RARS Accessibility Wrapper

Open Source Creator

A Python bridge for the RISC-V Assembler and Runtime Simulator. I built this to ensure visually impaired students have equal footing in low-level architecture courses, aiming to remove the friction and burnout that often pushes disabled peers out of STEM.
